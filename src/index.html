Benefits of using AIOL

Free

All In One Link is a project created for High Seas (Hack Club) and is free! That means that there are no subscriptions (yet) or locked features that require you to pay! Of course, running the servers for AIOL is costly, but you can help us by following our social medias- for free! That will show us that we actually have fans- and not bots!

Open Sourced

All In One Link is an open sourced project! This means the community can view the code, can create extensions for it and do whatever they want with the code!

Indie

We're a small, unregistered company. That means we're not trying to grasp as much money out of you or try to get your information sold!
